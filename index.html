One rating to help us all

If you are anything like me, you try avoid bad movies at all costs. A little bit of research is always helpful when you want to find out if you are about to watch a terrible flick. But ratings websites are so different! The IMDB scores sometimes seem a bit too optimistic (maybe too many people voting?), while Metascores can be a bit harsh (they are based on critic reviews, who tend to be harder to please).

What if you could "skew" the ratings to be a bit more closer to your taste? This is our goal!

Over the next three pages you will be asked to rate movies based on your perception (choose movies you know well, not necessaruly the ones you like most - you want it to be accurate). Your scores will be added to our algorithm and the ratings will be more tailored to you!
